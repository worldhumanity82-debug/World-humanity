Who We Are

About Us & Our Mission

🌍 World Humanity is an organization dedicated to humanity, compassion, and social service. We believe every life deserves dignity, love, and support — regardless of circumstance.

For over 21 years, this journey of service began with a simple belief: no one should feel alone in their struggles. Social Worker Loganathan has dedicated his life to bridging the gap for the abandoned and marginalized, creating an emotional connection that goes beyond basic charity.

💖 Our mission: "To extend love and support to every life in need."

What We Do

Our Services

Our services are filled with care and compassion ❤️. We walk beside those who have no one else.

🍛
Free Food (Annadhanam)

Removing hunger is our first duty. We provide free nutritious meals to those who need it most, because no one should sleep hungry.

🤝
Leprosy Assistance

Service and assistance to people affected by leprosy — offering dignity, medical support, and the warmth of human connection.

🕯️
Final Rites Service

"For those who have no one, we become their family ❤️"
With due respect and authority permissions, we ensure their bodies are handled with dignity, love, and compassion — giving a proper burial service.

🧠❤️
Mental Health Care

Care and support to people with mental health conditions, funded personally with deep compassion and commitment to healing.

📢
Social Awareness

Working towards a better society by creating awareness on important social issues — health, education, rights, and community harmony.
